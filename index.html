DNA+

DNA+ is a governance‑centric architectural framework designed to improve clarity, accountability, and coordination across complex organizations and systems. It focuses on how people, processes, and digital assets are defined, connected, and managed over time.

Rather than replacing existing tools or platforms, DNA+ provides a lightweight overlay that helps organizations understand ownership, responsibility, and status across operational boundaries. The approach is applicable to regulated environments, mission‑critical operations, and enterprises requiring strong traceability and disciplined change management.
