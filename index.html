DNA+

DNA+ is a governance‑centric architectural framework focused on improving clarity, accountability, and coordination across complex organizations and systems. It emphasizes how people, processes, and digital assets are defined, linked, and managed over time.

The framework is designed to complement existing tools and platforms by providing a lightweight, disciplined approach to ownership, responsibility, and change across organizational and operational boundaries. DNA+ is applicable to regulated environments, mission‑critical operations, and enterprises requiring strong traceability and controlled adaptation.
